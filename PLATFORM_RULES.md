# PLATFORM_RULES.md — Platform Rules Knowledge Base

> **Purpose**: Human-readable platform rules summary for agent reference.
> **Database Mirror**: Rules are also stored in `platform_rules` table with source, owner, confidence, and review dates.
> **Review Cadence**: Monthly for priority platforms, quarterly for secondary platforms.
> **Rule**: No rule may be used for auto-scheduling if stale beyond review SLA without human override.

## General Rules (All Platforms)

- Every post must be platform-native, not cross-posted identical text
- Every draft must receive a Reach Readiness Score before approval
- Black-hat tactics are prohibited: engagement bait, misleading claims, irrelevant tagging, hashtag stuffing, duplicated posts, artificial engagement
- Compliance and brand safety take priority over reach optimization
- Medical/health claims require evidence and appropriate disclaimers
