## Brand Identity

SmartLabs is a health-tech company offering advanced diagnostic services including blood testing packages. The brand communicates scientific credibility, accessibility, and innovation in healthcare.

## Target Audience

- Health-conscious individuals seeking proactive wellness monitoring
- Corporate clients interested in employee wellness programs
- Medical professionals and referral partners
- Tech-savvy users who value convenience and digital-first healthcare

## Tone

- **Professional** but approachable — not clinical or cold
- **Evidence-based** — claims backed by science, not hype
- **Empowering** — help users take control of their health
- **Clear** — avoid medical jargon unless speaking to professionals
- **Confident** — not aggressive or salesy

## Do

- Use clear, simple language to explain health concepts
- Highlight convenience, accuracy, and speed of results
- Share educational content about preventive health
- Use data and statistics when available
- Include clear calls-to-action
- Adapt tone to platform (professional on LinkedIn, conversational on Instagram)

## Don't

- Make medical claims without evidence or disclaimers
- Use fear-based health messaging
- Share patient data or identifiable health information
- Use excessive emojis or informal slang on professional platforms
- Make competitor comparisons without verified data
- Promise specific health outcomes

## Platform-Specific Voice

| Platform | Voice Adjustment |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Professional, thought leadership, B2B focus, data-driven |
| Instagram | Visual, educational, lifestyle-oriented, use Reels/Stories |
| X (Twitter) | Concise, engaging, conversation-starter, thread for depth |
| Facebook | Community-focused, local relevance, accessible language |
| TikTok | Educational entertainment, myth-busting, behind-the-scenes |

## Vocabulary

**Preferred**: wellness, diagnostics, proactive health, insights, accuracy, convenience, results, prevention, screening, innovation

**Avoid**: cure, treatment, diagnosis (unless by medical professional), guaranteed, miracle, secret, hack

## Examples

**Good LinkedIn Post**:
"Early detection saves lives. Our new comprehensive blood panel screens for 50+ markers in a single visit. Results in 24 hours. Book your screening → [link]"

**Bad LinkedIn Post**:
"🔬🔥 This ONE blood test could SAVE YOUR LIFE! Don't wait until it's too late! Doctors HATE this! #HealthHack #MiracleTest"
